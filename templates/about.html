Summary

At St. Philomena’s during his Surgical Residency Dr Ramraj has been trained and has worked with renowned Trauma, General, Laparoscopic, Gastro-intestinal, Vascular, Urology, Thoracic & Onco-surgeons. During his Fellowship in Minimal invasive surgery he was trained by a Surgical Gastro-eneterologist in:
  • Basic laparoscopy
  • Hernia surgeries
  • Advanced Laparoscopic & Thoracoscopic procedures including
  • Minimal invasive & open Upper gastro-intestinal surgeries
  • Minimal invasive & open Hepato-biliary pancreatic surgeries
  • Minimal invasive & open Colorectal surgeries
  • Single incision surgeries.
 During Fellowship at BGS Global Hospital in Minimal invasive surgery Dr Ramraj had the opportunity to be trained by a Surgical Gastro-eneterologist in:
  • Basic laparoscopy
  • Hernia surgeries
  • Advanced Laparoscopic & Thoracoscopic procedures including
  • Minimal invasive & open Upper gastro-intestinal surgeries
  • Minimal invasive & open Hepato-biliary pancreatic surgeries
  • Minimal invasive & open Colorectal surgeries
  • Single incision surgeries.
 At A.V Hospital & Vikram hospital for Metabolic & Bariatric surgery fellowship he was trained in more than 400 Bariatric surgeries including:
  • Sleeve gastrectomies
  • Banded sleeve gastrectomies
  • Roux-en-y gastric bypass
  • Banded Roux-en-y gastric bypass
  • Mini gastric bypass
  • Banded mini gastric bypass
  • Re-do bariatric surgeries
  • Ileal interposition
  • Reversal of Roux-en-y bariatric surgeries
  • Adolescent bariatric surgeries
